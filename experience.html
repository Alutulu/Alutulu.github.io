Work experience

In addition to my specialization in robotics during my studies, I did several internships, most of which enabled me to strengthen my knowledge of software development.

Web development

I did an internship at BizzDev, in Belgium, acquiring knowledge in the field of web application development.
I took part in the development of the SmartWaste application, which aims to alert municipalities when an illegal dump begins to form. This system is automated thanks to an artificial intelligence trained to detect waste on the ground, and embedded in a camera.
As I had the chance to work on every aspect of the application, this internship also brought me an introduction to machine learning, as well as soft skills in agile methodology.

  • Learn more about BizzDev

Software development

I did a technical internship at Dagoma 3D, a French start-up that designs and manufactures 3D printers. My purpose was to participate in the continuous improvement of a slicing software, Cura by Dagoma, using the Scrum method.
I mainly worked on the software's graphical interface using Python modules after migrating the project from Python 2 to Python 3. I also contributed to improving the package and the installer, taking into account user needs.

  • Learn more about Dagoma 3D

Production operator

My first internship was at a Bostik company's plant, producing adhesives for the construction, industrial and consumer sectors.
I worked as part of an operational team on adhesive packaging lines. I checked the conformity of the tubes of glue according to a production order.
What I take away from this internship is the skills I acquired, in particular the autonomy and responsibility I acquired in carrying out my tasks.

  • Learn more about Bostik
