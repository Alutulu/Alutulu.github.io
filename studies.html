President of IT Club

I'm currently President of the school's IT club, one of the student-run committees at IMT Nord Europe. The club has a dual purpose.
Club members are responsible for IT-related projects, such as web application design and server management. We also host various IT services useful to the school's students.

Autonomous robotics challenge

This year, we're working as a group on a mobile robotics challenge, which involves programming a robot to move autonomously in an environment with obstacles. It's a great way of learning and getting comfortable with OOP in Python.

Connected weather station

During my second year at IMT Nord Europe, I used Arudino components to design a WIFI-connected weather station. I was in charge of programming the microcontroller in C language, to hold sensor data, make statistics, visualize this data on an LCD screen, and communicate the information via WIFI so that it could be displayed on a web page.
